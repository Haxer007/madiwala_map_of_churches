✦ Our Story ✦

About Our Church

Zion AG Church is located on Maruthi Nagar Main Road, Madiwala — easily accessible from BTM Layout, Koramangala, HSR Layout, Electronic City and Bannerghatta Road.

Zion AG Church is your sacred space to encounter the Saviour of your soul. Come with an OPEN HEART — the peace, joy, strength, and transformation He provides are best experienced, not just explained.

📱 Stay connected beyond Sundays! Explore our sermons, updates, and upcoming events.

"Join us and discover what it truly means to live in the light of God's love and purpose!"
Rev. Ps. David Paul
Senior Pastor · Zion AG Church, Madiwala
Graduated from Southern Asia Bible College. Rev. David and his wife Pastor Sheeba, along with their two children, serve the Lord in the bustling hub of Madiwala, Bengaluru.
